Why This Matters

Coaches Make Decisions in Seconds.

Most Athletes Give Them Nothing to Work With.

Every year, college coaches review thousands of prospects. They don't have time to dig. They open a link, scan for 15 seconds, and decide whether to keep looking or move on.

Right now, most student-athletes have no control over what shows up when a coach searches their name. A MaxPreps page they never set up. An incomplete Hudl profile. Maybe nothing at all.

That's not a recruiting strategy — that's leaving one of the most important decisions of your athlete's life to chance.

And if a coach Googles your athlete's name right now — whatever the internet decides to show is what they see. You didn't build it. You didn't choose it. It just exists.
